Most companies perform penetration tests, code reviews and similar risk management exercises on their products. 
<br>However, this is a case of "bolting the stable doors after the horse has left". In reality, proper security engineering starts at the design phase of any product. 
Designing your product with security in mind is 1000 times less costly than doing it after releasing it. 
<br>At Agile Information Security, we have ample experience helping clients develop secure software. We help you analyse the data flows, components, authentication, authorization, data storage, encryption and other key parts of the product to ensure they are secure from the start, saving you millions of dollars in future problems.
