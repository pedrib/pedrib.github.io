We offer a comprehensive network monitoring service. Our approach is to deploy battle-tested sensors on your network to detect and prevent any malicious attacks on it. The sensors are constantly monitored to ensure any attack is detected quickly and dealt it. Our experienced consultants review the output of these sensors on a regular basis to ensure they are kept up to date with the latest vulnerability and hacking detection technologies.

Please note that this is not a 24/7 monitoring service, but more of an early alert system. If you would like to have a 24/7 monitoring service, we can assist you in selecting a supplier, or even to develop in house capabilities as we have done before for large clients.
